    Pyoro Clone

    December 2015

    A simple clone of the WarioWare game, Pyoro (https://www.mariowiki.com/Pyoro).

    I create this when I racing a friend, who was also creating the same project. At the time, we were both new to using SFML in C++, and so we were giving eachother tips on how to implement certain functionality as we go.

    In total, it took ~14 hours to complete.

    Hopson Land

    August 2015

    It was just a simple RPG game where you had the ability to roam around a little world, and then encounter enemies in certain parts where it would transision to an RPG turn-based-style battle system.
